About LCI

The Integrated Circuits Laboratory (LCI) at the Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC) is dedicated to advancing research and education in microelectronics. Located within the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, LCI focuses on CMOS circuit design, device modeling, and low-power technologies. Our work supports collaborations with institutions like PUC Peru, Udelar Uruguay, and UGA France.

Our Research

Advanced Compact MOSFET Model (ACM)

A charge-based model for precise MOSFET simulation, widely used in analog and digital circuit design.

Low-Voltage CMOS Design

Developing efficient circuits for low-power applications, bridging design and simulation.

Analog IC Design

Exploring innovative solutions for amplifiers, filters, and signal processing in CMOS technology.
