About LCI

The Integrated Circuits Laboratory at UFSC advances microelectronics through CMOS design and modeling, collaborating with global partners like PUC Peru, Udelar Uruguay, and UGA France. Based in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, we focus on innovation and education in integrated circuit technology.
